The Rongo Residency

Inspired by the Māori concept of Rongo—peace and harmony—this residency is a sanctuary for artists to create away from the bustle of everyday life. Designed as a retreat for deep reflection and artistic growth, the program connects residents with expert mentors while providing a serene environment to focus entirely on their craft. Each cycle rotates between disciplines, ensuring a curated experience for all participants.
